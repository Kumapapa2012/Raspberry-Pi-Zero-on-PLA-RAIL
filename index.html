What is PLA RAIL

It is Japanese famous toy train. See this for details. http://www.takaratomy.co.jp/english/products/plarail/what/index.html

Why Raspberry Pi zero?

Since its size may fit to PLA RAIL size. Actually it did. See Wiki GALLERY.
